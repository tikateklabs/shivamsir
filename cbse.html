ENGLISH- ICSE 10

Duration: 1 ½ hrs, 3 times a week.

What Is It?

The course concentrates on the Academic enhancement for the English Language & Literature as a subject in ICSE board. We help students prep for their boards, we have created a course for students to understand the foundation of the subject and perform better in their written paper for the English subject.

The Module

We have a module which caters to the need of students and the requirement needed to write essay type questions. The course concentrates on building the fundamental aspects required in writing, by enhancing student’s ability to construct better words and longer sentences to express their views regarding the short stories, poetry, and the novel "Merchant of Venice" which is taught in schools. We also investigate the students hold on the grammar and teach them the rules.

How Does It Help?

While some students find English learning to be enjoyable, there are many who struggle to cope with the grammar rules and intricate English. The crash course will provide valuable practice to the students for their boards.

How we do it?

All of the below is done with the objective of understanding the text and answering all type of questions that would appear in the ICSE examination.

LANGUAGE

  • Composition – argumentative, persuasive, narrative, descriptive, picture composition
  • Comprehension Letter Writing – Formal and informal
  • Grammar – from the basics – parts of speech

LITERATURE

  • Play - Merchant of Venice
  • Prose - Treasure Trove
  • Poetry - Treasure Trove
